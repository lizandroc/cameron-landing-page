Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cameron Cruz ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit this website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name and email address when you subscribe to the newsletter, submit the contact form, or schedule a call. We may also collect basic technical data (browser type, pages visited) to understand how the site is used.

Cookies

We use essential cookies required for the site to function, and — with your consent — non-essential cookies for analytics. You can accept or reject non-essential cookies via the consent banner. Your choice is stored in your browser and can be changed by clearing your site data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your messages and scheduled call requests
  • To send you emails you've signed up for (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To operate and improve this website

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We use trusted service providers to operate the site — including Cloudflare (hosting and data storage) and Resend (email delivery) — who process data on our behalf.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
